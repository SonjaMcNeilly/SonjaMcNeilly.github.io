    What camera equipment do you currently usde for your Photography?

    Currently, the camera I use for my photography is the Fujifilm XS-10. The lens I shoot with is the XC 15-45mm lens. Also, when I go out shooting photos, I store my camera in my Peak Design Everyday DSLR Sling V2 6L.

    Would you recommend your camera for beginners?

    Yes, I would recommend the camera I use for beginners. The Fujifilm XS-10 was my first camera, and I think it was fairly easy to get used to the settings of the camera. The camera also comes with film simulations to enhance the look of your photos straight from your camera. The grip on the camera is also extremely beneficial in terms of ergonomics.
